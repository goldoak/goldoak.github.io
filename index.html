Jiaxin Wei

I am a second-year PhD student in the Smart Robotics Lab at the Technical University of Munich, supervised by Prof. Stefan Leutenegger. My research focuses on 3D vision, mapping, and object-level perception. I am also passionate about bridging the gap between the physical and virtual worlds. I earned my Master's degree from ShanghaiTech University, where I worked in the Mobile Perception Lab under the supervision of Prof. Laurent Kneip.
